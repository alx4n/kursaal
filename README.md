# Kursaal
Casino meets roguelike in this dungeon crawl through the eras of gambling.

### Genre
Roguelike, dungeon crawler

### Target Audience
Teens, roguelike and dungeon crawler fans

### USP
Emphasis on comedy, travelling through the ages of gambling for a unique visual environment

## Product Design
### Player Experience and Game POV
You play as Ernest, an older man who has just lost all his retirement money at Kursaal, the casino of the ages.
The game is in 2D with a top-down perspective, ala The Legend of Zelda.

### Visual and Audio Style
The visuals are in a sketchy, Flash-game like style, with thick outlines and cartoony proportions.
The sound effects will be brash and in-your-face, reminiscent of early 2000s cartoons. The music will change drastically as you descend the casino, matching each visual era.

### Game World Fiction
The casino at large draws in many customers, cursing them to lose their money to feed it. Few have braved the journey to get their funds back, with fewer ever returning.

### Monetization
The game will be pay-to-play, with no microtransactions.

### Platforms, Technology, Scope
This game is meant to be played on PC or console. The style is 2D and built using Godot's 2D engine.

## Game Systems Design
### Core Loops
Start out a run with a gun, fight through several floors of enemies while picking up items and new weapons along the way, to eventually make your way to the core of the casino and take back your money.

### Objectives and Progression
Each floor contains a boss, blocking the way to the next. Beating it allows you to go to the next floor. Several floors must be cleared to beat the run.
Completing runs and doing specific tasks within them unlocks metaprogression in the form of new character, item and weapon unlocks.

### Game Systems
Floors will be generated with random chains of rooms, filled with enemies. Each floor will contain items and a boss at the end.
Item and weapon pickups will also be randomized for each run, as usual for roguelikes.

### Interactivity
The player can move, shoot and perform a melee attack. The simple controls exist in balance with the chaos the items, sounds and visuals can cause.
